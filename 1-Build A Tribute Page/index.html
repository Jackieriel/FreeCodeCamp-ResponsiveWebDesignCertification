John Hindemith

A musician, father, thinker and dev..

Biography:

  • Jonathan was born in Nova Friburgo - Rio de Janeiro - Brazil. He is a multi-instrumentalist, has studied piano and as a self-learner has developed skills as a music producer and some string instruments over 10 years.
    In 2009 he has begun to learned music productions techniques by self-learning and produced some electronic music singles and has performed as a DJ in electronic music events, doing DJ-sets and live acts.
    Along the way, he also was awarded in 3rd place on an online Remix World Contest promoted by the band Celldweller.
    As a listener, he has always been in search of an electronic sound that would provide and melodic line improved, and despite a lot of quality content in the world scene, he never found something that had his particularities as a listener.
    After some years in pause and faced with this personal need, in early 2016 he came back to model his artistic project, writing poems and recording improvisations as piano, voice, and guitar.
    Upon returning in early 2017, faced with some impasses in his personal life, the desire arises to present some of his engraved "informal" creations to friends and family.
    With a very positive reaction, a great feeling arises that many people could identify with their truths and essence.
    "Work and produce all ideas and records of your experience and emotions from real life, turning them into music."

    These songs are fragments of his life transcribed in scores.
